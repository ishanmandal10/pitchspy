PitchSpy – AI Competitor & Idea Validator

PitchSpy is an AI-powered tool designed to help founders, builders, and product thinkers analyze competitors, validate ideas, and generate pitch insights in seconds instead of hours.

🧠 Overview

Before launching a product or preparing a pitch, founders often spend hours researching competitors and validating their ideas.

PitchSpy automates this process using AI.

Simply provide competitor URLs or describe your idea, and PitchSpy delivers:

Structured competitor insights
Market positioning analysis
Idea validation with demand signals
A basic investor-ready pitch
